Task:
Formulate 5 criteria by which the system will determine relevant business industries (branchen) in Germany to search for potential B2B clients for this product. The answer must be used as an instruction in the next step — the actual selection and ranking of industries.

Answer requirements:
– provide exactly 5 criteria;
– each criterion must be no longer than 120 characters (excluding importance);
– assign each criterion an importance from 1 to 100, where 1 is the most important;
– criteria must be sorted by importance (from 1 to 100);
– criteria must describe principles for selecting industries, not the industries themselves;
– the answer structure must be stable and suitable for further use.

Answer format (must be strictly followed):

[IMPORTANCE] – Criterion
[IMPORTANCE] – Criterion
[IMPORTANCE] – Criterion
[IMPORTANCE] – Criterion
[IMPORTANCE] – Criterion

Forbidden:
– mentioning any industries;
– providing examples;
– performing the selection itself;
– formulating more or fewer than 5 criteria.
