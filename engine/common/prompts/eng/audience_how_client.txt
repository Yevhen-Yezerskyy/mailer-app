Task:
Formulate 5 criteria by which the system will determine the relative probability of a successful deal with a specific company. These criteria will be used in the next step to rank the found companies by likelihood of conversion.

Answer requirements:
– provide exactly 5 criteria;
– each criterion must be no longer than 120 characters (excluding importance);
– assign each criterion an importance from 1 to 100, where 1 is the most important;
– criteria must be sorted by importance (from 1 to 100);
– criteria must describe principles for assessing deal probability, not the companies themselves;
– the answer must be structured and suitable for direct use as an instruction in the next step.

Answer format (must be followed strictly):

[IMPORTANCE] – Criterion
[IMPORTANCE] – Criterion
[IMPORTANCE] – Criterion
[IMPORTANCE] – Criterion
[IMPORTANCE] – Criterion


Forbidden:
– mention specific companies;
– provide examples;
– perform the assessment itself;
– formulate more or fewer than 5 criteria.
